Built on trust,

The Heritage Basket grew from a simple belief: good food should be honest, fresh, and within reach. What began as a trusted stall in Lokoja has become a name families and businesses rely on for their grains, oils, fish, produce and daily needs.

Whether you're cooking for your home or stocking a shop, we bring the same care to every order - fair prices, quality you can taste, and service that treats you like family.
